Regional Sales & Profitability Analysis
Technical Lead: Vincent Ta

Project Overview
This project involved the development of an interactive Tableau dashboard to track global performance metrics across multiple product categories. The objective was to perform a multi-regional margin attribution analysis to isolate drivers of profitability and identify operational trends.

Data Integrity & "Detective" Findings
A critical component of this analysis was maintaining data integrity. During the visualization process, a significant outlier was identified and addressed:
Missing Data Reconciliation: The 'Sales Trend' visualization shows a sudden decrease in 2018 sales; this was identified as a missing data issue rather than a decline in company performance.
Performance Projections: Despite the 2018 data gaps, overall sales are projected to outperform the 2017 fiscal year totals.

Key Business Insights
The analysis focused on identifying high-value segments and product categories to inform strategic decision-making:
Regional Outperformance: Mexico was identified as the highest-performing region, generating both the highest total revenue and the largest profit margin overall in 2018.
Product Profitability: A cross-category analysis revealed that Colorbuds and Action Figures were top-tier performers, ranking in the top five for both sales volume and net profitability.
